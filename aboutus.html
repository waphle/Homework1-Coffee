Who are Jebediah and Stephen?

Jebediah Longtree was born on April 1, 1993 to immigrants from Cofilend, Iceland, a place known for their amazing coffee. Stephen Harvester, born on April 20, 1994 to a family with a history dating back to the American Revolutionary War, took upon brewing coffee after tasting the horrid fake coffee at his college's cafeteria. The two met when Stephen posted a discussion thread on WhyCoffeeNowadaysSuck.com, and Jebediah responded to Stephen's post. The two talked back and forth, and low and behold, the two happened to just live just a few blocks away from each other! They met and started to create their own brews, committing to create the best tasting coffee in the nation's captial while staying as hip and green as possible, and the rest is history.
